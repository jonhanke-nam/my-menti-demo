- `presenter:join`: after validation, queries sessions table for active session, stores on `socket.data.sessionId` and `activeSessions` map
- `participant:join`: reads sessionId from `activeSessions` map with DB fallback, stores on `socket.data.sessionId`
- `presenter:next`: passes `activeSessions.get(roomCode)` to `getAggregatedResults`
- `participant:vote`: includes `sessionId: socket.data.sessionId ?? null` in INSERT, passes sessionId to `getAggregatedResults`
- `presenter:end`: sets `endedAt = now()` on session row, deletes from `activeSessions` map

### Step 6: Join Route (`server/src/index.ts`)
- Added import of `sessions` and `isNull`
